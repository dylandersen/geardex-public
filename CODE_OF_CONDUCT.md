# Code Of Conduct

This repository is dedicated to respectful, constructive collaboration.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and professional
- Focus feedback on product behavior and user impact
- Assume good intent

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks
- Doxxing or sharing private information
- Spam or deliberately disruptive behavior

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove content that violates these expectations and may restrict repeat offenders.

For security-related issues, use private reporting as documented in `SECURITY.md`.
